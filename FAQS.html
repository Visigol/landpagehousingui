Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Automated Check-In system work?

Our system integrates with smart locks (TTLock) and Google Sheets to automatically generate and send access codes to guests via email and WhatsApp.

Is the system compatible with my smart locks?

Our platform supports TTLock-enabled smart locks. If you have a different brand, contact us to check for compatibility.

Is there a monthly fee?

No! Unlike other solutions, our system is a one-time purchase with no recurring costs.

Can I customize the system for my business needs?

Yes! Our system is fully customizable to suit hotels, Airbnb hosts, vacation rentals, and long-term property managers.

How do guests receive their access codes?

Once a reservation is processed, the system automatically sends an email and WhatsApp message with the access code.

What happens if a guest has issues with check-in?

We provide automated customer support options, and property managers can manually issue codes if needed.

How secure is the system?

We use secure authentication methods and integrate with TTLock for encrypted key management.

How can I purchase this system?

If you are interested in acquiring our **Automated Housing Check-In System**, the first step is to contact us expressing your interest. We will then schedule a **brief video consultation** to better understand your specific needs and business model. This allows us to tailor a **fully customized version** that aligns with your operational workflow and guest management processes. During the consultation, we will walk you through how the system works, its features, and the available package options. The implementation timeline depends on the selected package and the complexity of the required customizations. Development and deployment typically range from 1 to 6 weeks, ensuring that the system is fully set up, tested, and ready for seamless operation by you and your guests.
